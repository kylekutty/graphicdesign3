Visualizing Health Value of Food without Effects of Deceptive Marketing Tools

In recent years, our society has grown more health conscious in our eating habits, most likely due to rising obesity rates and the discovery of several health complications that often accompany said disease. However, eating healthy is not as straight forward as we might think. Marketing tricks in the design of food packaging can alter the health value we perceive in a given food, and with this deception taking place all throughout the grocery store, it can be almost impossible to select actually healthy foods.

This project aims to shine a spotlight on this deception and to help us begin to see through it. When you press start you will be prompted with two quizzes. One will be based solely on the packaging of the food and the other solely on the nutritional information of that food.

start

Once you complete the two quizzes, click submit to reveal two abstract visualizations of the perceived health on the left and the actual health on the right. The more inconsistencies that you see signify more dishonesty on the part of the marketing of the food's health. Feel free to return to the quizzes to test more foods or discover which type of motion is being affected by each factor.
